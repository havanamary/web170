WELCOME

I was born in Havana, Cuba and arrived to the states when I was only four days old. Growing up in Miami, Florida I was an active member of the community supporting organizations which promote social justice. In addition, I volunteered for various elderly homes and elderly care organizations. While supporting myself through school I worked as a legal assistant and administrator for attorneys in an array of specializations. I attained an Associate in Arts Degree specializing in Architecture in 2013 and shortly thereafter moved to Seattle, Washington.

While living in Seattle, I have attained a Certification in Paralegal services. However, I yearned to serve the community hands on. I sought opportunities to work in the nonprofit sector. For the last 3 years I worked for a major nonprofit organization. During which time I earned a Certification in Grant Writing and was able to contribute my skills towards a request for funds that was granted in full.

Having much success in my pursuits and goals, I am currently working on developing my web design skills enrolled in a certificate program at Seattle Central College. On my free time I enjoy cooking, hiking, camping, and exposing new friends to savory foods, music, and arts of Cuban-American culture.
